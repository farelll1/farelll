Adoption Process

We've developed a comprehensive process to ensure our Savannah Kittens go to prepared and loving homes.

Preparing For Your New Savannah Kitten

Animals bring some of the greatest joy we can ever imagine. Our purpose is to help spread a little bit of that joy, to each customer we serve at our facility, we believe you don’t just bring home a kitten, you bring home a new family member.

  • Before welcoming home your new Savannah kitten, it’s essential to prepare a safe environment. A designated small and comfortable room or space should be selected where your Savannah kitten can spend at least the first one to two weeks, until they are comfortable to explore additional parts of your home. Basic necessities such as food, water, and litter box are also needed. We always send your kitten home with a starter kit with sample food.

    Please be aware of the scammers offering kittens for ridiculous amounts, you will pay at least $1,500 to $25,000 depending on the generation you are Interested in.
    With thoughtful preparation, your Savannah kitten will have a great time and a good experience growing up with you.

Application & Approval

Complete our detailed application form to help us understand your experience, living situation, and preparedness. We review each application carefully to ensure our Savannah Kittens go to suitable homes.

Education & Preparation

Once approved, we provide comprehensive educational materials and a preparation checklist. We'll schedule video consultations to ensure your home is properly set up before your kitten arrives.

Reservation & Deposit

Once you've selected your desired Savannah Kitten, a 50% refundable deposit secures your reservation. The remaining balance is due 24 hours after pick up or shipping.

Health Certification

Prior to picking up/shipping, your Savannah Kitten receives a complete health examination from our veterinarian. We provide all health certificates and documentation.

Pick Up/Safe Transport

We schedule pick up/shipping date always glad to welcome everyone. We use specialized animal transport services with climate-controlled conditions. Your Savannah Kitten travels with a nanny, provides premium hospitality.
